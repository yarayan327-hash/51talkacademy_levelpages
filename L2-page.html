Narrative Mastery

51Talk Academy L2 shifts from "What is this?" to "What happened?". It aligns with Saudi G7-G9 standards and Int'l G3-G4 curricula.

Level 2 Milestones:

  • Narrate historical events and personal stories using Past Simple.
  • Compare technology and products using Superlatives.
  • Write cohesive paragraphs for reviews, blogs, and emails.
  • Discuss global issues like environment and animal protection.
